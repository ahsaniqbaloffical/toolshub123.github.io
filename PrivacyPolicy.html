Privacy Policy

At Toolshub123, your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data while you use our services.

Data Collection

We believe in absolute transparency. Toolshub123 is designed to be a "Zero-Data" platform. Most of our tools process information directly in your browser using JavaScript.

  • No Account Required: You can use all tools without signing up.
  • No Personal Tracking: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Client-Side Processing: Your SEO audits, financial data, and health inputs never leave your device.

Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience and analyze basic site traffic. This helps us understand which tools are most helpful to our users.

Google Analytics:

We may use anonymous analytics to track page views. No personally identifiable information is stored.

Theme Preferences:

A small piece of local storage is used on your device to remember if you prefer Dark Mode or Light Mode.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. Toolshub123 is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to read their policies upon visiting them.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out to us directly.
