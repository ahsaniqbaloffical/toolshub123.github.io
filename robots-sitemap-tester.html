Why Use This Tool?

Robots.txt and sitemaps are critical for SEO. Misconfigurations can block crawlers or hide important pages.

  • ✔ Ensure search engines can access your content.
  • ✔ Detect blocked resources that may affect rankings.
  • ✔ Verify sitemap URLs are valid and up to date.
  • ✔ Save time with instant analysis and exportable reports.

FAQs

  • Do I need both robots.txt and sitemap? Yes. Robots.txt controls crawler access, while sitemaps help search engines discover your pages.
  • Can I have multiple sitemaps? Absolutely. Use a sitemap index file to reference multiple sitemaps.
  • Why avoid blocking CSS/JS? Search engines render pages like browsers. Blocking assets can hurt rankings.
  • What is a good compliance score? Aim for 90+ to ensure your site is crawlable and well‑structured.

Tips for Better SEO

  • ✅ Keep your robots.txt simple and avoid unnecessary disallows.
  • ✅ Update your sitemap regularly to reflect new content.
  • ✅ Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
  • ✅ Monitor crawl errors and fix broken or redirected URLs quickly.
