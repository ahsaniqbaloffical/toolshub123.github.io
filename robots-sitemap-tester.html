FAQs

  • Do I need both robots.txt and sitemap? Yes, robots.txt controls crawler access, sitemaps help discovery.
  • Can I have multiple sitemaps? Yes, use a sitemap index and multiple “Sitemap:” lines.
  • Why avoid blocking CSS/JS? Search engines render pages; blocking assets can hurt rankings.
