FAQs

  • Do I need both robots.txt and sitemap? Yes, robots.txt controls crawler access, sitemaps help discovery.
  • Can I have multiple sitemaps? Yes, use a sitemap index and multiple “Sitemap:” lines.
  • Why avoid blocking CSS/JS? Search engines render pages; blocking assets can hurt rankings.

About This Tool

The Robots.txt & Sitemap Tester is designed to help webmasters, SEO specialists, and developers quickly validate two of the most important files for search engine optimization. These files guide search engines on how to crawl and index your site.

With this tool, you can:

  • ✔ Detect errors and warnings in your robots.txt directives.
  • ✔ Verify that your sitemap.xml contains valid and accessible URLs.
  • ✔ Instantly calculate a compliance score to measure overall health.
  • ✔ Export results to CSV for reporting or share a quick summary with your team.

By simplifying technical checks, this tool ensures your site remains crawlable, indexable, and optimized for better visibility in search engines.
