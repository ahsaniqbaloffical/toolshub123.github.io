      Why Use a Free Heading Structure Checker?

      Properly structured headings (<h1> through <h6>) are essential for SEO optimization, accessibility, and user experience. Search engines like Google rely on heading hierarchy to understand your content. A clear structure improves keyword relevance, readability, and helps your site rank higher in search results.

      Our Heading Structure Checker instantly analyzes your HTML code, counts heading levels, detects missing or duplicate tags, and highlights skipped levels. This ensures your content follows SEO best practices and is optimized for both users and search engines.

      Key Benefits of the Heading Structure Checker

      • ✅ SEO Audit: Detect missing H1 tags, multiple H1s, or skipped heading levels.
      • ✅ Keyword Optimization: Check if headings contain your target keywords.
      • ✅ Accessibility: Improve screen reader compatibility with logical heading hierarchy.
      • ✅ Export Reports: Download results in CSV format for easy sharing with your team.
      • ✅ Free Online Tool: No signup required — analyze headings instantly.
